About Us

The Most Preferred Hotel in West

Eko Hotels & Suites is the most preferred hotel in West Africa, and it is all
about the right mix! Located in the heart of Victoria Island, we offer our
clients a perfect blend of business & leisure amenities with dining and
recreational options delicately infused in one amazing space. We crown
all these with services that meet the highest international standards.

Overlooking the new Eko Atlantic City and Atlantic Ocean, it is just a 10-
minute drive to the City Centre and only 45minutes away from the Airport.

Our hotels are designed for your comfort and convenience. Your security
is our primary concern and you will find our customer care second
to none
